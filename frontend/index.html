Car Hire Services

Hire premium vehicles at flexible and transparent rates. Whether for business, travel, or special occasions, Amprovans Motors offers reliable and well-maintained vehicles ready for you.

How Our Pricing Works

Our daily hire rates are calculated based on the value of each vehicle to ensure fairness and consistency. Typically, the daily rate is approximately 0.7% of the car’s market value.

This means higher-value vehicles (such as luxury models) have higher daily rates, while more economical cars remain affordable. Final pricing may vary slightly depending on demand, rental duration, and special arrangements.

Late Return Policy

Vehicles returned after the agreed return date will incur a late fee of 20% of the daily rate per extra day.

We recommend timely returns or early communication with our team to avoid additional charges.

Getting Around the Website

The homepage contains quick access links to all services:

  • Buy a Car – Opens the vehicle listings section
  • Hire a Car – Takes you to the rental section
  • Sell Your Car – Opens the car submission form

These are clickable sections—clicking them scrolls or navigates you directly to that feature.

Buying a Car

Go to the Vehicles section from the homepage or navigation menu.

  • Browse available cars or use the search bar
  • Apply filters (Make, Model, Year, Price) to narrow results
  • Click a car card to view full details and images

Once you find a car, use the provided contact option to proceed with purchase.

Hiring a Car

Navigate to the Car Hire section.

  • Select a vehicle you want to hire
  • Choose your rental start and end dates
  • The system automatically calculates the total cost

Note: Daily rate = 0.7% of the car value.

Late returns are charged 20% of the daily rate per extra day.

Selling Your Car

Open the Sell Your Car section and fill in the form.

  • Expected Price – Your asking price
  • Car Details – Basic information about your vehicle
  • Images – Upload up to 10 clear photos (max 7MB each)

After submission, your listing is reviewed before being published.

Search & Filter Tips

  • Use keywords like car name, engine type, or transmission
  • Combine filters for more accurate results
  • Clear filters if no results appear
