Car Hire Services

Hire premium vehicles at flexible and transparent rates. Whether for business, travel, or special occasions, Amprovans Motors offers reliable and well-maintained vehicles ready for you.

How Our Pricing Works

Our daily hire rates are calculated based on the value of each vehicle to ensure fairness and consistency. Typically, the daily rate is approximately 0.7% of the car’s market value.

This means higher-value vehicles (such as luxury models) have higher daily rates, while more economical cars remain affordable. Final pricing may vary slightly depending on demand, rental duration, and special arrangements.

Late Return Policy

Vehicles returned after the agreed return date will incur a late fee of 20% of the daily rate per extra day.

We recommend timely returns or early communication with our team to avoid additional charges.
